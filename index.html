Our Process Journey

Discovery

A meeting with you to discuss your ideas & needs and start the collaboration with you and your business. We aim to truly understand what it is you wish to get as an outcome and build a picture that then helps our nerds put the design in place.

Design

Once we have your design brief, then let the nerding begin! we will be busy working through your new digital marketing content or coding your new fully functional and snazzy website , ready for you to sign it off!

Go Live

We go live! - yes, we have pressed the button on your idea's once you have signed off on them. We will contiue to watch user activity to ensure its all functioning correctly and that your marketing is reaching to the desired audience.

Maintain

Once we have a steady Go Live! then we are on hand should you need us to help maintain your new content and tools. Please speak to us at your Discovery and how we can support you on an ongoing basis.
